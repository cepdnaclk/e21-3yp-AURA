Capabilities

AURA Features

Every capability that makes AURA a next-generation restaurant experience.

Pan & Tilt Face Tracking

Dual MG996R servo-driven head mechanism combined with OpenCV Haar Cascade face detection. AURA actively follows the customer seated at its table, creating a natural "alive" interaction feeling.

OpenCV GPIO Zero MG996R

Autonomous Touch Ordering

Customers browse the full menu, customise items, and place orders directly on the 7" capacitive touchscreen. Orders transmit to the kitchen instantly via MQTT — no waiter required.

PyQt5 MQTT SQLite

Voice Interaction

Natural language voice recognition for customers who prefer speaking over tapping. AURA listens, understands menu queries, and responds — making dining accessible for all ages and preferences.

SpeechRecog pyttsx3

Smart RGB Ambient Lighting

User-controlled mood lighting with three presets — Dining (warm white), Reading (cool white), and Party (dynamic colour cycle). Controlled via touchscreen with instant MQTT relay to LED strips.

WS2812B MQTT

Multi-Language Support

Eliminates language barriers between customers and staff. AURA's interface dynamically switches between Sinhala, Tamil, and English — ensuring every guest can order comfortably in their own language.

i18n Python

Wireless Battery Operation

10,000 mAh Li-ion battery pack with smart power management keeps AURA running a full restaurant shift. Zero cables, zero wiring infrastructure — drop it on any table and it works immediately.

10,000mAh Wi-Fi
